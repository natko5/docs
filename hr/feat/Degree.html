Treebank Statistics (UD_Croatian)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Cmp, Pos, Sup.

13097 tokens (15%) have a non-empty value of Degree. 5558 types (32%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. 2445 lemmas (28%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Degree. The feature is used with 2 part-of-speech tags: hr-pos/ADJ (10161; 12% instances), hr-pos/ADV (2936; 3% instances).
