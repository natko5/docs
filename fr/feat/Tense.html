Tense: tense

Tense should only occur on verbs in French, with one of the four following values: Past, Pres, Fut or Imp.

Treebank Statistics (UD_French)

This feature is universal. It occurs with 4 different values: Fut, Imp, Past, Pres.

38844 tokens (10%) have a non-empty value of Tense. 7036 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. 2013 lemmas (6%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Tense. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: fr-pos/VERB (30339; 8% instances), fr-pos/AUX (8502; 2% instances), fr-pos/NOUN (3; 0% instances).

30339 fr-pos/VERB tokens (84% of all VERB tokens) have a non-empty value of Tense.
