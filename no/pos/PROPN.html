PROPN: proper noun

#####Definition A proper noun is a noun (or nominal content word) that is the name (or part of the name) of a specific individual, place, or object.

In Norwegian, proper nouns do usually not inflect for morphological properties like gender, number, etc. Proper nouns are furthermore written with a capital letter.

#####Examples * Kari, Ola * Oslo, Bergen
