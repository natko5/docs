iobj: indirect object

The indirect object of a (verbal) predicate is the nominal which is the dative object of the verb. The relation iobj is used for objects that are not direct objects. It occurs only when there is a dobj or ccomp in the clause.

She gave me a raise
iobj(gave, me)
