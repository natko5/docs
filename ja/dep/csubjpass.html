csubjpass: clausal passive subject

The dependency type csubjpass is used for clausal subjects of passive verbs.

言っ た の が 悔やま れる 。 \n say PAST that NOM regret PASSIVE .

auxpass(悔やま, れる)
aux(言っ, た)
mark(言っ, の)
case(言っ, が)
punct(悔やま, 。)
