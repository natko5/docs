case: case marking

The case relation is used for linking prepositions to their heads:

O văd pe Maria . \n Her see-I PE Mary . (I can see Mary.)
case(Maria, pe)

Pun cartea pe masă . \n Put-I book-the on table .
case(masă, pe)
